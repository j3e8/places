Privacy Policy

Kulana has a strict privacy policy wherein personally identifying data is not shared with nor sold to third parties. Some anonymous data is collected during regular use of the site/app, however, at no time is that data made public nor linked to private data of any kind. Additionally, Kulana does not store any sensitive information for its patrons.

Kulana respects privacy laws and does not intentionally collect data of any kind from minors under the age of 13.

If you believe your privacy has been compromised by use of Kulana's sites and/or services, please contact us at support@kulana.social.
